What Samla does

Three things. Done perfectly.

📥
One unified inbox

Every review from Google, Reco.se, Facebook and Trustpilot in one scrollable feed. Filter by platform, star rating, or "needs reply." No more tab-switching.

✦
AI reply drafts

Click any review and get a professional reply in seconds — in Swedish or English, matching your business tone. Edit, copy, done. Responding to a 1-star review takes 30 seconds.

📊
Monday morning digest

Every Monday at 08:00 you get an email: new reviews, average rating trend, and the 2 reviews that need your attention most. Stay on top of your reputation in 90 seconds a week.

How it works

Set up in 5 minutes.

01
Connect your profiles

Log in with Google and connect your Business Profile in one click. Add Facebook, Reco.se, and Trustpilot in under 5 minutes. No technical setup needed.

02
See everything at once

Your last 30 days of reviews appear instantly in one feed. You'll probably see a review you never knew existed.

03
Reply in 30 seconds

Click any review, get an AI-drafted reply, make any edits you want, and copy to the platform. We'll add one-click publish to Google in the Growth plan.
